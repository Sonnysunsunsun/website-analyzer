Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan anytime? +
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the credit will be applied to your next invoice.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.
What happens if I exceed my monthly limit? +
If you reach your monthly analysis limit, you can either wait for the next billing cycle or upgrade to a higher plan. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit so you're never caught off guard.
Is there an API rate limit? +
Yes, API requests are rate-limited based on your plan. Professional plans get 100 requests per minute, while Enterprise plans get 500 requests per minute. Custom limits are available upon request.
